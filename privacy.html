Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This privacy policy applies to the mobile application Bussen, developed and distributed by the creator of this app ("we", "our", "the developer").

1. No Data Collection

The Bussen app does not collect any personal data, device information, analytics, or usage data. No information is transmitted to the developer or to any third parties.

2. No Third-Party Services

The app does not use advertising networks, tracking tools, analytics services, SDKs, or external APIs that might collect user data.

3. Local Functionality Only

All game actions and logic run locally on the user's device. The app does not require an internet connection and does not interact with remote servers.

4. Target Audience

The Bussen app is intended for users aged 18 and older. This app does not provide content directed at children.

5. Changes to This Policy

If the privacy policy is updated in the future, any changes will be published on this page.
