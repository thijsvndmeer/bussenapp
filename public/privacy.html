Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

This privacy policy applies to the mobile application Bussen, created and maintained by the developer (“we”, “our”, “the developer”).

1. Overview

The Bussen app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data to show relevant ads, prevent fraud, and measure ad performance. The developer does not collect or store any personal data directly.

2. Information Collected by AdMob

AdMob, operated by Google LLC, may collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version, device identifiers)
  • IP address
  • Approximate location (derived from IP)
  • App interaction data and crash data
  • Advertising ID (used for personalised or non-personalised ads)

You can read Google’s full data policies here:
Google Ads Policy

3. Personalised vs. Non-Personalised Ads

Depending on your region and settings, Google may show:

  • Personalised ads – based on your ad profile
  • Non-personalised ads – based only on contextual data

The app forwards your consent choice to Google through the User Messaging Platform (if required by law).

4. Children

The Bussen app is intended for users aged 18 and older. It is not directed at children and does not show child-targeted advertising.

5. Third-Party Links

The app does not contain external links or features that redirect to third-party websites.

6. Data Security

The developer does not store any user data. AdMob handles and protects data according to Google's security standards.

7. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Updates will be posted on this page.
